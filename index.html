About Us

We are a small game development team from Hacettepe University, working on Farm to Table as our graduation project. The team consists of 2 developers and 1 designer, focusing on hybrid simulation experiences.

Project Description

Farm to Table is a hybrid simulation game that combines peaceful farming mechanics with fast-paced restaurant management. You grow your own ingredients in the morning and use them to serve to your customers in the afternoon.
